Bread Toast


Ingredients :

  1. bread
  2. butter
  3. onion
  4. potato
  5. green chilli
  6. salt
Preparation time : 10 min

Process :

  1. step1: boil potato
  2. step2: Finely chop onion and green chilli
  3. step3: mix with potato and salt
  4. step4: spread butter on bread
  5. step5: add the mixture evenly
  6. step6: toast on a pan or toaster until the bread turns golden and crispy
